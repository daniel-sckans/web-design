Assignment Seven

Create a website with pages demonstrating 8-, 20-, and 32-cell grid layouts (as well as a fourth page with your choice of grid cells) in the style of Raster Systeme (see below).

Parameters

Raster Systeme is a classic Graphic Design book by Swiss designer Josef Mueller-Brockmann. Look at the examples in the sections marked "Type and picture area with n grid fields" (there are three). You may want to also view other parts of the book, such as "Construction of the grid".
Use the CSS grid system to construct a four-page website demonstrating 8-, 20-, and 32-cell grid layouts (and one page with your choice of grid cells).
You may choose your number
You may use any of the web programming and design techniques we have discussed to date (e.g. images, multiple pages, display types).

Evaluation Criteria

The design of the individual pages; composition; the way the eye is led through the page.
Varied use of CSS grids.
The inclusion of other media in the page such as photos, images, and shapes.
The site should be live and fully functioning at your Github site.
