Assignment Four

Part One: Play Web Games

Parameters:

Visit daniel-sckans.github.io/web-games and beat each of the web games there eight (8) times.
Use your Surface, and bring your Surface with you to class (we will verify your number of wins).
Your score doesn't matter, only the number of times you win the game.

Part Two: Create an Abstract Site

Parameters:

From scratch, create a one-page site comprised entirely of colors and shapes.
Use CSS properties such as display (e.g. "display: block"), position (e.g. "position: fixed"), width, height, top, right, bottom, left, background-color, padding, margin, and so forth.

Part Three: Prepare for Midterm Critiques

Parameters

You will be creating a site of your own choosing for the midterm.
Begin considering things like its name, function, intent, general look and feel, and so forth.
