Assignment Ten

Create a wireframe for your final.

Parameters

The wireframe should include the general structure for your web page, including your links between pages and general layout.
It should be constructed in such a way that it will help you lay out your final.
It should be completed using Balsamiq Cloud or Adobe XD; optionally, you may provide photographic documentation of sketches you've done.
Your wireframe should be exported and then uploaded to our course Drive folder (link will follow via email).

Evaluation Criteria

The ability of the user interface to elegantly communicate with the viewer.
The consideration for significant UI problems relevant to your site (e.g. linking between pages; the the layout of pages).
Details and examples of content to be added (e.g. the addition of colors, images, or fonts).

Resources

Don't Make Me Think, by Steve Krug, is a popular and lighthearted account of UI principles; please read the first two chapters.
